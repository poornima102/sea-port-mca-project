About Sea Port

Gateway to Global Trade

Vizhinjam International Sea Port is a state-of-the-art deepwater, multipurpose port located on the southern tip of India. Our port is designed to handle the largest container ships, bulk cargo, and passenger vessels, making it a vital hub for international shipping and logistics.

  • Modern container and cargo handling facilities
  • Efficient customs and logistics support
  • Secure and sustainable port operations
  • 24/7 support for exporters and importers
