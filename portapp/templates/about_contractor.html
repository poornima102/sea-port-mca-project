About the Contractor Portal

The Contractor Portal at Vizhinjam International Seaport is designed to streamline and simplify all contractor-related activities. This portal provides contractors with a secure and user-friendly platform to manage tenders, submit applications, track work status, and access important notifications and agreements.

  • View and Apply for Tenders: Browse available tenders and submit your applications online.
  • Track Application Status: Monitor the approval status of your tender applications in real time.
  • Download Agreements: Access and download contract agreements once your application is approved.
  • Work Status Updates: Submit and view updates on the progress of your assigned projects.
  • Profile Management: Update your company and contact details to keep your profile current.
  • Alerts & Notifications: Stay informed about new tenders, approvals, and important announcements.
  • Support: Reach out to our help desk for any assistance or queries.

Our goal is to ensure transparency, efficiency, and ease of use for all contractors working with the seaport. If you have any questions or need support, please contact our help desk or email us at contractor.support@vizhinjamport.in.
